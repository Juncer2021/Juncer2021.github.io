这里是 Weaver 的个人网站，打算用来发布一些我认为重要或有价值的东西。质量纯凭个人水平，仅作留档使用。本贴持续施工中……

Weaver 这个名字出自《Shadow Slave》。

I call myself Weaver.

Not out of some grand delusion of pulling the strings of fate. Before I became who I am, I saw the world for what it truly is, and survived the trials in its deepest shadows. I know better than anyone that true power is never a gift. It’s something you draw out from the void, thread by thread.

A weaver. Someone who brings order from chaos, who stitches a faint light into the dark.

I write dreams with code and trap light with pixels. Every project, every piece of art, every line of text, is a thread I pull to forge an armor for myself. Not to hide from the darkness, but to walk deeper into it, steadily, in search of a brighter light.

This is not fate. This is a choice. A choice to imagine another possibility when everyone else accepts reality as it is, and to weave it into existence.

This is who I am. This is my Dream Realm, my tapestry.

“I am the Demon of Fate, first heir of the Unknown, unwelcome wanderer of the Void, weaver of a sinister Spell, of a Nightmare — slain by a mortal, slain by Fate.”
